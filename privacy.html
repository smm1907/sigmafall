PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: January 2026

Sigma Fall is a mobile game developed and published by the creator of Sigma Fall.
Your privacy is extremely important to us. This game is designed to be fully playable without collecting, storing, or sharing any personal data.

1. Information We Collect

Sigma Fall does not collect any personal data, including but not limited to:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Device ID
  • IP address
  • Location
  • Usage data
  • Crash logs
  • Analytics data

The game works entirely offline and does not require any user accounts, login, internet connection, or external services.

2. How We Use Data

Because no data is collected, no data is processed, stored, sold, or shared.

3. Third-Party Services

Sigma Fall does not use any third-party services such as:

  • Advertisements (AdMob, Unity Ads, etc.)
  • Analytics (Firebase, Google Analytics, Unity Analytics)
  • Social logins
  • Payment processors

4. Children’s Privacy

Sigma Fall does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since the game collects no data at all, no child data can be stored or processed.

5. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or stored, there is no risk of your data being accessed, leaked, or misused.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the new version will be posted on this page.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: samimbaydar@hotmail.com
